Who am I

I’m Sangeetha Srinivasan, but you can just call me Sangii.

I'm an interdisciplinary designer working on learning at the crossroads of technology, design, and art. I'm a Student living in India. Over the last decade, I've had the chance to help my college validate, prototype, and design. In college, even though I started out as a Biomedical Engineering major, I've got an interest in Interactive Digital Media,  mass communication, and art.

I'm working on small interactive web projects, mostly in WordPress and HTML, doing some Portraits. Once I was done with school, I was ready. I always seek to learn new things.I want to learn how real technology was being designed and built.
